USDT Withdrawal FAQ

Frequently asked questions about USDT withdrawals

What is the difference between BEP20 and ERC20?

BEP20 is the token standard on Binance Smart Chain, offering lower transaction fees and faster processing. ERC20 is the token standard on Ethereum, with higher fees but wider compatibility with wallets and exchanges.

How long does a USDT withdrawal take?

BEP20 withdrawals typically process within 1-12 hours. ERC20 withdrawals may take 1-24 hours due to Ethereum network congestion. Processing times may vary based on network conditions.

What are the withdrawal fees?

We charge a 0.5% processing fee plus network fees: 1 USDT for BEP20 and 5 USDT for ERC20. The minimum withdrawal is 10 USDT for BEP20 and 50 USDT for ERC20.

Can I cancel a withdrawal request?

Withdrawal requests can be cancelled within the first 30 minutes after submission if they haven't yet been processed. Once processing begins, cancellations are not possible.

What happens if I send to the wrong address?

Cryptocurrency transactions are irreversible. Always double-check your wallet address before submitting a withdrawal. We are not responsible for funds sent to incorrect addresses.
