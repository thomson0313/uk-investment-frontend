Overview of Advanced Manufacturing

先进制造业概述

Advanced Manufacturing (AM) represents the integration of innovative technologies and methodologies into manufacturing processes. The UK is at the forefront of this industrial revolution, leading in smart factories, robotics, and sustainable production systems.

Our robust R&D ecosystem, combined with world-class engineering talent, positions the UK as a global hub for manufacturing innovation and excellence.

Key Areas of Focus:

  • Industrial digitalisation and Industry 4.0
  • Additive manufacturing (3D printing)
  • Robotics and automation
  • Sustainable manufacturing
  • Materials science and engineering

先进制造业（AM）代表了将创新技术和方法论整合到制造流程中。英国处于这场工业革命的前沿，在智能工厂、机器人和可持续生产系统方面处于领先地位。

我们强大的研发生态系统，加上世界一流的工程人才，使英国成为制造创新和卓越的全球中心。

重点领域：

  • 工业数字化和工业4.0
  • 增材制造（3D打印）
  • 机器人与自动化
  • 可持续制造
  • 材料科学与工程

Why choose the UK to develop advanced manufacturing?

为什么选择英国发展先进制造业？

World-class R&D

The UK ranks among the top nations globally for research and development in manufacturing technologies. Our world-renowned universities and research institutions collaborate directly with industry to drive innovation.

Key strengths and sector support

The UK's manufacturing sector is diverse and resilient, with particular strengths in aerospace, automotive, pharmaceuticals, and electronics.

Access to talent and skill base

The UK boasts a highly skilled workforce with expertise in engineering, technology, and advanced manufacturing processes.

世界级研发

英国在制造技术研发方面位居全球前列。我们世界知名的大学和研究机构直接与行业合作，推动创新。

关键优势与行业支持

英国制造业部门多元化且具有韧性，在航空航天、汽车、制药和电子领域具有特殊优势。

人才与技能基础

英国拥有高技能的劳动力，在工程、技术和先进制造流程方面具有专业知识。

UK Advanced Manufacturing Development Fund

英国先进制造业发展基金

The UK Advanced Manufacturing Development Fund represents the government's commitment to fostering innovation, creating high-quality jobs, and securing the UK's position as a global manufacturing leader.

With significant investment in research and infrastructure, including £1.2 billion dedicated to the Advanced Manufacturing Research Centre (AMRC), we're building the manufacturing capabilities of the future.

英国先进制造业发展基金代表了政府对促进创新、创造高质量就业机会以及确保英国作为全球制造业领导者地位的承诺。

通过对研究和基础设施的重大投资，包括专门用于先进制造研究中心（AMRC）的12亿英镑，我们正在建设未来的制造能力。
