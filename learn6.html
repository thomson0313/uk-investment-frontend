Professional and Business Services Overview

专业与商业服务概述

The UK Professional and Business Services (PBS) Industry represents one of our most dynamic and essential economic sectors, providing the expertise, infrastructure, and strategic support that enables businesses of all sizes to thrive in a complex global marketplace. As a global hub for professional excellence, the UK delivers world-class services across legal, financial, consulting, and business support domains.

With a reputation for quality, integrity, and innovation, the UK PBS sector generates over £200 billion annually and employs more than 4.5 million people across the nation, making it one of the largest and most diverse professional services markets in the world.

Key Sectors:

  • Legal services - International law firms and legal expertise
  • Accounting and auditing - Financial services and compliance
  • Consulting - Strategic, management, and technical advisory
  • Business support services - Administrative and operational support
  • Marketing and PR - Brand development and communication strategies
  • Human resources - Talent management and organizational development
  • Research and development - Business intelligence and innovation services

英国专业与商业服务（PBS）产业代表了我国最具活力和最重要的经济部门之一，提供专业知识、基础设施和战略支持，使各种规模的企业能够在复杂的全球市场中蓬勃发展。作为专业卓越的全球中心，英国在法律、金融、咨询和商业支持领域提供世界级服务。

凭借质量、诚信和创新的声誉，英国专业与商业服务部门每年产生超过2000亿英镑的收入，并在全国范围内雇佣超过450万人，使其成为世界上最大和最多样化的专业服务市场之一。

关键领域：

  • 法律服务 - 国际律师事务所和法律专业知识
  • 会计与审计 - 金融服务与合规
  • 咨询 - 战略、管理和技术咨询
  • 商业支持服务 - 行政和运营支持
  • 市场营销与公关 - 品牌发展和沟通策略
  • 人力资源 - 人才管理和组织发展
  • 研究与开发 - 商业情报和创新服务

UK Professional and Business Services Development Programme

英国专业与商业服务发展计划

The UK Professional and Business Services Development Programme represents a comprehensive strategy to strengthen our position as a global leader in professional expertise and business advisory services. Through targeted investment, skills development, and international promotion, we're building an ecosystem where professional services businesses can thrive and scale globally.

Our programme includes significant support for professional qualifications, digital transformation in service delivery, export promotion, and initiatives to enhance diversity and inclusion across the sector. With dedicated funding for skills development and innovation, we're ensuring the UK remains at the forefront of professional services excellence.

The Professional and Business Services Council brings together government and industry leaders to address key challenges and opportunities, from adapting to digital disruption to maintaining the UK's competitive advantage in global markets.
