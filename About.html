About the Department for Business and Trade

Helping businesses invest, grow, and export across the UK and worldwide

Who We Are

The Department for Business and Trade (DBT) is at the heart of the UK Government. Our aim is to help overseas businesses, investors and buyers do business successfully in the UK, leveraging the UK's political stability and position as a global leader.

We bring together expertise from across government to provide comprehensive support for international businesses looking to establish or expand their presence in the UK market.

Our Services

For Businesses Expanding into the UK

We'll help you with every aspect of growing your business. We have a wide range of digital tools to help you save time and money during your expansion process.

For Investors Allocating Capital in the UK

From family offices to sovereign wealth funds, we work with all types of investors to smooth your investment journey with tailored advice, insights and introductions.

For Buyers Importing from the UK

We can help your business find the right products and services by showing you what's available in the UK. We can also provide financial support and make import regulations easier to understand.

Our Global Network

With offices in over 100 countries worldwide, our international network provides on-the-ground support and expertise to help your business succeed in the UK market. Our teams understand local business cultures and can provide insights that make your market entry smoother and more successful.

Why Choose the UK?

The UK offers a unique combination of political stability, world-class talent, innovative ecosystems, and business-friendly regulations. As the world's 5th largest economy, the UK provides access to European markets and beyond, with a strong legal system that protects intellectual property and investments.

关于我们

商业贸易部（DBT）是英国政府的核心部门。我们的目标是利用英国的政治稳定性和全球领导地位，帮助海外企业、投资者和买家在英国成功开展业务。

我们汇聚了政府各领域的专业知识，为希望在英国市场建立或扩大业务的国际企业提供全面支持。

我们的服务

对于希望扩展英国业务的企业

我们将在业务增长的各个方面为您提供帮助。我们拥有各种数字工具，帮助您在扩展过程中节省时间和金钱。

对于希望在英国分配资金的投资者

从家族办公室到主权财富基金，我们与各类投资者合作，通过量身定制的建议、见解和引荐，使您的投资之旅更加顺畅。

对于希望从英国进口的买家

我们可以通过向您展示英国提供的产品和服务，帮助您的企业找到合适的选择。我们还可以提供财务支持，并帮助您更好地了解进口法规。

我们的全球网络

我们在全球100多个国家设有办事处，我们的国际网络提供实地支持和专业知识，帮助您的企业在英国市场取得成功。我们的团队了解当地商业文化，可以提供见解，使您的市场进入更加顺利和成功。

为什么选择英国？

英国提供了政治稳定性、世界级人才、创新生态系统和有利于商业的法规的独特组合。作为世界第五大经济体，英国提供了进入欧洲及其他市场的通道，并拥有保护知识产权和投资的强大法律体系。
