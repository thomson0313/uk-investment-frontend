Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about investing in the UK

What sectors are prioritised for investment in the UK?

The UK prioritizes investment in eight key sectors: Advanced Manufacturing, Clean Energy & Green Tech, Life Sciences & Healthcare, Digital Technologies & AI, Creative Industries, Financial Services, Aerospace & Defense, and Infrastructure & Construction.

What support does the UK government provide to investors?

The UK government offers comprehensive support including investment advisory services, market research, regulatory guidance, assistance with site selection, and access to various grants and incentives such as R&D tax credits and innovation funding.

How long does it take to set up a business in the UK?

The process of setting up a business in the UK can be completed in as little as 24-48 hours for most company structures. However, the overall timeline depends on factors such as business type, sector regulations, and the complexity of your investment.

What are the tax incentives for foreign investors?

The UK offers various tax incentives including R&D tax credits, the Patent Box regime (reduced corporation tax on patented innovations), Enterprise Investment Scheme (EIS) tax relief, and capital allowances for qualifying investments.
